Short guidelines for traveling Cox’s Bazar

Cox’s Bazar is a town on the southeast coast of Bangladesh. It’s known for its very long, sandy beachfront, stretching from Sea Beach in the north to Kolatoli Beach in the south.

  • Vast sea with a soft bed of sand and rows of Zhaowban. If anyone goes to Cox's Bazar, he/she will want to visit the beach in the morning and afternoon. A beautiful sea beach with blue water and continuous roaring is the Cox’s Bazar. Cox’s Bazar is the longest sea beach in this beautiful world. This report is for those who want to visit with their families.
  • Maheshkhali, Kutubdia, Sonadia, Matar Bari, Shahpori Island, and Saint Martin have made Cox's Bazar more interesting and eye-catching for tourists. Mata Muhuri, Bankkhali, Reju, Kuhliya, and Naf river cross over this district. Tourism, forestry, fish, dry fish, snails, and sand with silica made Cox’s Bazar as top most visiting spot for the tourist.
